GBar

Minimal timer toolbar for Mafia.org

How to Use

  • Left-click buttons to navigate to action pages
  • Right-click buttons to start/restart timers
  • Timers persist across page navigation
  • Multiple timers run simultaneously
  • Toggle sound with ♪ icon
  • Click ⚙ for settings (colors, info)
  • Click × to hide toolbar

Preset Timers

  • Whack: 57 seconds
  • Mug: 2 minutes
  • Flight: 15 minutes
  • Train: 30 minutes
  • Scavenge: 22 minutes
  • Old Man: 30 minutes
  • Bandage: 5 minutes
  • Custom: Enter your own seconds
